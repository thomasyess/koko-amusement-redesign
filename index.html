Redesign rationale

A realistic 2026 evolution.

This concept keeps KOKO's recognisable logo, warm red-orange energy, game-card model, location network, games, prizes and family-friendly positioning. The change is in hierarchy, usability and conversion.

01

Location-first homepage

Most visitors need to decide where and when to visit. Store hours and directions now appear before promotional content, reducing Google Maps drop-off.

02

Stronger first message

The hero immediately communicates multiple Sydney venues, late trading and suitability for both families and young adults.

03

Browseable game categories

Categories turn a long machine list into an understandable experience and give social campaigns useful landing targets.

04

Membership explained

Benefits are described before the CTA, making the reusable card and saved-ticket value obvious to first-time visitors.

05

Trust and community

Reviews and social content show the venue through customer activity, supporting word-of-mouth discovery without changing the business model.

06

Mobile conversion

Large tap targets, horizontal card browsing and a persistent location action suit traffic from Maps, Instagram and TikTok.
